Written and structured by me. Built by Claude Code. Next.js, hosted on Vercel, measured with PostHog. The repo is public, including the instructions the agents work from.

The copy was drafted against a style guide whose main job is keeping AI writing tells out of the text. An agent-built site checked for sounding too much like an agent: that's the point. The agents do the labor. The judgment is mine.

Machines are welcome to read this site. robots.txt allows the major AI crawlers by name, and [llms.txt](/llms.txt) gives them the map.

Analytics are cookieless: PostHog runs with memory persistence, the site sets no cookies, and there is nothing to ask consent for.
