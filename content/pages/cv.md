## Professional summary

Marketer who builds. 15+ years in B2B marketing across fintech, SaaS and sportstech, and a founder who ships with AI. I co-founded HeadCoach, where I owned go-to-market and ran the product, and before that I was the first marketer at Lightyear, a B2B fintech, positioning a technical product for skeptical finance and accounting buyers. I write everything myself, work AI-first, and measure marketing by pipeline. I wound HeadCoach down in June 2026 and chose to bring that experience into a team rather than start again alone.
