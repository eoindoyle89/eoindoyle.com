A mental performance app for competitive athletes, sold to clubs, schools, and colleges in the US and UK.

I shipped the first version myself with no-code tools, then hired the engineering team that rebuilt it properly. Along the way we won grant funding, places on two accelerators, and our first paying customer, a US club. I built the systems around the product too: agent-run outbound that created the sales pipeline, customer-facing analytics dashboards, and the context design for the in-app AI agent, written with our sport psychologist.

I wound the company down in June 2026, with strong product validation but not the revenue scale to sustain a founding team. I chose to bring that experience into a team rather than start again alone.
